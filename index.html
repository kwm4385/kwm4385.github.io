Visual Sorter

Program developed to help visualize the workings of different sorting algorithms. It includes many sorting strategies and allows users to modify the array size, sorting speed, and provides away to translate the sort into an audio pattern played back in sync with the sort.

Java, Swing

AutoDJ - In Progress

As my current project, the goal of this web application is to allow users to create their own music playlist from sources such as YouTube, Vimeo, and SoundCloud. They will be able to share a link to a custom page through which others can make song requests and vote for songs others have requested. The site will be useful for those who want to share music with others either in person such as at a party, or for those who livestream content such as video games on the internet to thousands of viewers.

Python, Django, HTML/CSS, Javascript, jQuery, AJAX
